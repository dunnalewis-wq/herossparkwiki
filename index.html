This is the begining of a wiki page for my game, A Hero's Spark

Please feel free to check around, look at the images and what they are. The game itself is still a massive work in progress but I truly love the idea of people being able to see the progress so far.

The forest of forgotten

The forrest of death is known to be the only part of land you can never return from. There is foretold to be a supernatural gateway through this land, yet no one has been able to reach it.

The dimension of affray

This is a dimension very little people know of. It's filled of hate, fury, destruction, affray, and so much more. If you ever find yourself in this dimension with another being, pray that you know how to fight better than your enemy. For that is the only way to get out.

Shirohi's home

This is where the fiery hero, Shirohi lives. He's almost never home, always out helping others, going on walks, or just chopping trees.
